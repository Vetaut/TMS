We Are...
The Penn State Thespians!
Thespians is the oldest, continuously running student organization on campus. Every year, we present a number of student-run productions, including two main stage musicals, a children's show each semester, and an annual cabaret.

Our History

Now in our 120th anniversary season, our club is dedicated to producing quality theatre for the community.

Community Involvement

Thespians is proud to give back to the community through our children's show, MasquerAIDS, and THON™. It is part of our mission to be an active member of our local community.

Get Involved

Become a member of The Penn State Thespians through our main stage productions, children's show, MasquerAIDS, and more! We're always excited to see new faces in club.
