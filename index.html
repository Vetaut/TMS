The curtain rises on a present-day musical theatre fanatic eager to tell you about her favorite Broadway musical -- "The Drowsy Chaperone." She's the ultimate fan and "Drowsy" is her guilty pleasure. As she begins listening to the rare cast recording, the show cleverly and magically blooms to life, telling the hilarious tale of a pampered Broadway starlet and her debonair fiance, an overzealous producer, a dizzy chorine, the Latin lover and a couple of bumbling gangsters. Ruses are played. Hi-jinks occur. And the plot spins everyone into musical comedy euphoria.
